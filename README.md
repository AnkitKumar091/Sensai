# 🧠 Sensai – Your Path to Career Success

**Sensai** is a modern, all-in-one platform that helps users become *industry-ready* by offering personalized interview quizzes, AI-powered resume and cover letter builders, weekly industry insights, and a comprehensive skill analysis dashboard.

Whether you're a fresher preparing for your first role or a professional aiming to level up — Sensai provides the tools you need to succeed.

---

## 🚀 Features

- ✅ **Role-Based Interview Quizzes**  
  Practice quizzes tailored to your selected job role to help simulate real interview conditions.

- 📄 **Smart Resume & Cover Letter Builder**  
  Instantly generate professional resumes and personalized cover letters based on your skills and preferences.

- 📊 **Weekly Industry Insights**  
  Stay updated every 7 days with the latest trends, technologies, and job market insights.

- 🧠 **Personal Strengths & Improvement Tracker**  
  Get detailed feedback on your strong areas and suggestions on where to improve.

- 📌 **Actionable Feedback Dashboard**  
  Visualize your progress and understand where you stand in your career journey.
